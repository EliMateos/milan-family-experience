# Milan Family Experience

Interactive surprise invitation game for a family trip to **Milan**.  
A ~20 minute guided journey with questions, clues, living-room challenges and a final reveal in the form of a boarding pass.

> A web experience created as an interactive invitation to a surprise trip to Milan.  
> A 20-minute emotional journey filled with questions, clues, family challenges, and a final reveal in the form of an airplane ticket.  
> Elegant, festive, and fully self-contained — just open it in your browser and enjoy.

## Features

- Step-by-step interactive flow (wizard style)
- Global 20-minute countdown
- Clickable chips (radio + checkbox)
- Progress bar
- Recap screen with all answers
- Final reveal screen showing the Milan voucher image
- Pure **HTML + CSS + JavaScript**, no build step and no external dependencies (besides Google Fonts)

## Live usage

1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Connect your laptop to the TV.
3. Sit together as a family and let one person be the “captain” who clicks.
4. Follow the rounds, complete the challenges and reveal the destination at the end.

## Customisation

- Edit the copy in **index.html** to adapt texts, language or jokes.
- Swap `assets/vale_milan.png` for your own boarding-pass-style voucher.
- Tweak colours and layout in **styles.css**.
- Adjust step logic, validation or timing in **script.js**.
